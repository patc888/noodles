Webpages

Lots of webpages that demonstrate how to call to the backend to get data. Lots of examples of layouts and how to display data.

Name Description
news_1 Shows top stories from an rss new feed.
flickr_1 Shows images whose tags match a search term.
geoplace_1 Shows information about a location. E.g. "Palo Alto" or "SFO".
maps_1 Shows the location of a place (e.g. "SFO" or "Palo Alto") on a Google map.
